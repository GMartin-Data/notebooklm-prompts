## Style visuel

Coller dans le champ "Describe a custom visual style" :

```text
Infographic style, Swiss design aesthetic, professional color palette with navy and gold accents, clean grid layout, minimal decoration.
Show metrics and KPIs as dashboard panels with trend indicators.
```

## Focus IA

Coller dans le champ "What should the AI hosts focus on" :

```text
Target audience: [ROLE] making decisions about [TOPIC/INITIATIVE]

Focus on:
- Lead with the bottom line (recommendation or key insight)
- Quantify impact with metrics and KPIs
- Frame information in terms of business outcomes
- Surface risks and mitigation strategies
- Provide clear decision points or next steps

Skip:
- Technical implementation details
- Methodology explanations
- Granular data breakdowns
- Caveats that don't affect the decision

Tone: Direct, confident, action-oriented
```
